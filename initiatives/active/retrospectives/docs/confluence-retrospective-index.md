## Welcome to the New Retrospective Model

This is your central hub for everything related to the new retrospective process.

**What changed:** We moved from SRE-led, presentation-based retrospectives to **team-led, collaboration-based** retrospectives with framework-guided quality assessment.

**Why it matters:** Faster retrospectives (45 days vs 72 days), better quality, more team ownership, and systemic detection of recurring issues.

## Metrics We're Tracking

### Process Metrics
- Retrospective lead time (target: <45 days, current: 72 days)

- % completed within SLA

### Quality Metrics
- Average quality score by team
- % retrospectives meeting >70% threshold
- Pre vs post-assessment improvement

- Incident recurrence rate
- Action item completion rate

### Common Issues

|-------|----------|
| Can't identify faulty asset | Contact Process Eng — they'll help identify |
| Multi-team ownership dispute | EMs collaborate; escalate to VSL if needed |
| Missing incident data | Check RDINC, Slack channel, Rootly; ask SRE |
| SLA at risk | Contact Process Eng immediately |
| Score stuck <70% | Request additional working session or Process Eng support |
| VSL approval delayed | Process Eng will follow up and escalate if needed |
